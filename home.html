Meditation is a practice that involves focusing the mind to achieve a state of mental clarity, relaxation, and heightened awareness. It often involves techniques such as mindfulness, concentration, or guided imagery to calm the mind and body. The goal can vary, from reducing stress and improving concentration to achieving a deeper understanding of oneself. Meditation can be practiced in various forms, including sitting quietly, repeating a mantra, or engaging in mindful movement
